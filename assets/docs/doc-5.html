pfSense (Netgate 2100) | Router-on-a-stick | Default-deny policy

DESIGN PRINCIPLES

All inter-VLAN traffic is denied by default; only the rules below permit traffic. Each VLAN is segmented so that the Attack and Victim networks cannot reach the Management or SIEM planes. Isolated VLANs (Victim, Attack) have no internet access, enforced implicitly by the absence of any WAN-permit rule under the default-deny policy.
